About MythBusters for Kids

Welcome to MythBusters for Kids, where we explore over 100 amazing myths and legends from around the world in a fun and exciting way!

Join our team of curious animal friends as we investigate whether sharks can really smell a drop of blood from miles away, if you can turn invisible by wearing a special cloak, and whether dragons might have actually existed!

Each myth is turned into a fun adventure story where we search for clues, conduct experiments, and discover the truth together!

Our Myth Rating System

TRUE - The myth is supported by evidence

BUSTED - The myth is not supported by evidence

MAYBE - The myth could be true under specific circumstances
